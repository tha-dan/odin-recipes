Basic Burger

A basic and delicious burger

Sometimes less is more. With just 5 ingredients and 15 minutes of cooking time, this flavorful recipe is ready to be topped and sauced in any way you like!

Ingredients

  • 1 large egg
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1/2 cup fine dry bread crumbs
